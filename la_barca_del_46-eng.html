la barca del 46: archivo plural del 46sna
—the 46's boat, 46sna's plural archive—
curator
museo nacional de colombia, sede bogotá
bogotá, colombia
2022

exhibition of processes and outcomes from various mediation and training activities of the 46th national artists’ salon, carried out throughout the magdalena river basin in 2022. the exhibition featured a cultural program aimed at activating the space so that it was not only for exhibition but, especially, for creation and gathering.

exhibition text:
la barca del 46 reconstructs a narrative that flows, like the river, from san agustín to barranquilla, gathering local experiences of what the 46sna has been. this space invites participation in a dialogue between the exhibitions and the different mediation and training activities that have taken place throughout the magdalena river basin.
